About This School

The University of Manila was founded by the late Pio Ramos in response to the lack of accessible education in the Philippines. Recognizing that education is the key to empowering individuals and communities, Ramos envisioned an institution that could provide high-quality learning opportunities for students from all backgrounds.

Since its founding, the University of Manila has been committed to academic excellence. The school offers a wide range of programs that nurture not only academic growth but also the moral and social development of its students. Through dedicated faculty, modern facilities, and a supportive learning environment, the university prepares its students to become leaders, professionals, and responsible citizens.
